HONDA CBR 300R

In production since 2015 as a successor to the Honda CBR250R, the 2021 Honda CBR300R increases displacement to a 286cc liquid-cooled single-cylinder four-stroke engine producing 30.4 hp (22.7 kW) @ 8,500 rpm and 20 lb⋅ft (27 N⋅m) @ 7,250 rpm.
